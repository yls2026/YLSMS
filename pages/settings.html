Start New Year

Attendance & Fees don't have a year column — the Jan-Dec columns always mean "this year". When a year ends, use this to archive the current Attendance and Fees sheets (e.g. "Attendance 2026") and clear Jan-Dec back to blank for everyone, ready for the new year. Member records are never affected.

  • Copy the current Fees sheet to
